PROCEDURE

  • First cut the carrots into reactagular shapes(almost half of your finger size),then cut the cauliflowers into small florets,cut the paneer into cubes.
  • Now blanch the vegetables (carrots ,cauliflower ,peas).
  • Meanwhile soak some rice and then cook it till 70% done in salted water along with some whole spices(cardamom,cinnamon,cloves,bayleaf).
  • Take a pan and add some oil to it and fry the paneer till golden brown ,then fry the vegetables with some tumeric and salt in the same oil.
  • Take out the vegetables in a bowl after frying.
  • Now in the same pan add some ghee then add bay leaf,dried red chilli and whole spices to it.
  • Now add one roughly chopped onion ,few garlic cloves, giner paste ,2 roughly chopped tomatoes and some cashews.
  • Add salt ro it and cover and cook till tomatoes are completely soft.
  • Now remove the bay leaf and whole spices and make a smooth paste of it with the help of water.
  • Now heat a pan and add ghee to it, when it gets heated add spices to it(tumeric,red chili powder,coriander powder,cumin powder)and saute in low flame.
  • Now add the paste to it and cook it till it releases oil ,then balance the salt and add bit of sugar, mix it.
  • Then add the fried vegetables and paneer to it and mix it well.
  • Add some garam masala and a dollop of butter to it and cover it for 2-3 mins in medium flame.
  • Add the precooked rice and mix well then add very little warm water and keep it covered till the rice is perfectly cooked.
  • At the end check the seasoning and add some chopped coriander,mix it.
  • Your paneer pulao is ready , tastes best when served hot.
